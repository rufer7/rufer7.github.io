About me

I was born and grown up in Moosseedorf a village in the agglomeration of Bern. After primary school and a subsequent apprenticeship in electronics I studied computer science at the Bern University of Applied Science.

Since my studies I'm working as a Software Engineer. From August 2013 until May 2015 I worked at mimacom ag in Bern and since June 2015 I'm working at d-fens GmbH. I like working with open source technologies and frameworks like Spring, AngularJS, Docker,... and so on. My main strengths are the development of web applications and RESTful-Backend-Services.
